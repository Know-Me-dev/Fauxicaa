Privacy Policy

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for www.Fauxica.com. It will notify you of the following:

Information Collection

We collect personal information such as name, email address, shipping and billing information when you place an order or engage with our content. Information about your computer browser, IP address, and pages visited is also collected.

Information Use

The information we collect is used to operate our website, fulfill orders, manage subscriptions, and provide relevant content. We may use the information to improve our products, services, and website functionality.

Log Files and Cookies

Like most websites, we use log files and cookies to collect data. Cookies are small data files stored on your computer. Log file data includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), timestamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This data helps analyze trends, administer the site, and track user movement.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise share your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, marketing, and conducting business. Service providers are bound to safeguard all user data.

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

Children's Online Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover we have collected info from a child, we will promptly delete it.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy. Contact us with any questions or concerns regarding this policy.
