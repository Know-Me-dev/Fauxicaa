FAQs

What shipping methods do you use?
We use USPS and FedEx for domestic shipping within the United States. For international shipping, we use DHL and FedEx.
+
Do you offer free returns?
Yes, we offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. To start a return, visit our returns portal or contact us.
+
What is your return policy?
We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. All we ask is that the items are unworn, unwashed, and still have the tags attached. To start a return, visit our returns portal.
+
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship globally to over 50 countries worldwide! Delivery times vary based on your location. Contact us if you need expedited international shipping.
+
Can I exchange an item for a different size?
Absolutely! We want you to love what you purchased. You can exchange an item for a different color or size within 30 days of receiving your order. Log into your account to initiate the exchange.
+
Do you offer discounts or coupon codes?
Yes, we occasionally provide discounts and coupon codes to our newsletter subscribers and social media followers. We'll send out emails and social posts whenever we're running a promotion. You can also check on our website for any currently active coupon codes before checkout.
+
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary based on your location. For domestic shipping within the US, orders typically take 2-5 business days to arrive via USPS or FedEx Ground. International delivery times can take 5-10 business days on average. Contact us if your order hasn't arrived within the estimated time frame.
+
What is your sizing like?
We aim for our sizing to be as inclusive as possible. We provide detailed size charts for each clothing category to help you find your best fit. If you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up. You can also reach out to us for personalized sizing recommendations.
+
